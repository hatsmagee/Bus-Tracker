🚌 About this dashboard
A live map of every Hele-On Bus in service right now, using the County's official transit feed. Polls 22 routes every 10 seconds, stores GPS pings in SQLite, and runs a small transformer that learns typical arrival times for each stop from history.
🎨 How to read it
Bus icons sit at the bus's latest GPS position, pointing the way it's heading. Blue stops are GTFS-registered stops along active routes — click or tap one to see approaching buses and historical arrivals. Golden arrows ahead of a bus show the next stretch of route it's about to travel.

🧠 The predictor
A 12-token single-head transformer (~704 parameters) trained online every time a bus reaches a stop. Inputs are cyclical time-of-day, distance to next stop, scheduled delta, speed, and the historical average mph for that route. Outputs are ETAs to the next 5 stops.
